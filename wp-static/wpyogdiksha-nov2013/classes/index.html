Classes

General public classes

I’m currently based in Ealing, West London.  I’m looking to start teaching general classes in this area soon.

Private classes

One-to-one classes have the benefit of focusing directly on your specific needs and goals. It is a great way to get to know and understand your body whilst addressing your particular tendencies and weak areas. One-to-one is the traditional way of teaching which allows the student to practice exactly according to their personal needs under close guidance of an experienced teacher.

Family class or a small group class can also be arranged.

These classes can be offered at the comfort of your home and necessary equipment will need to be available.

Corporate classes

Corporate yoga classes are a fantastic way to boost the work morale and well being of employees. Yoga helps build strength, stamina, balance and flexibility, whilst creating a balanced mental state with strong sense of vitality and health.

I offer lunchtime and evening classes on-site. Suitable space will need to be arranged to accommodate all the participants. Basic yoga equipment will also need to be provided.

Private and corporate classes are priced at £60 for 1 hour class or £45 for 45 min class.
